In Lieu of Flowers

Understanding and Support

Mental Health is important, and when battling things like Bipolar Disorder, it's important to know yourself and how best to navigate the world around you. Josh struggled for his entire life, trying to find that delicate balance between mania and depression to feel some kind of normalcy with his brain. When things become difficult it's easy to turn to things such as alcohol as a way to cope, and it's important to recognize the signs and make positive changes in your life. I've left a number of resources available here and urge anyone reading this; if you or someone you love is struggling, please, get the help that's needed before it's too late.
